About me

A Traditional Artist, Graphic Designer and Graphic Designer Located In Philippines

Hi, I’m Jay Mark also known as mockingartjay, a traditional artist, llustrator and Graphic Designer from the Philippines. I specialize in crafting detailed, creepy, and whimsical illustrations for books, graphic novels, and beyond. My work primarily uses black-and-white or grayscale, which I find adds a unique depth and character to my art. I combine my artistic skills with development to create engaging interactive experiences that enhance storytelling. Inspired by my surroundings, I aim to blend intricate details with imaginative elements, creating visuals that both intrigue and enchant. Thank you for checking out my work—I look forward to exploring new creative possibilities with you!

My Services

Ink Illustrations

I specialize in ink illustration, transforming client ideas into detailed artwork using black ink fine liners. My work primarily involves traditional techniques, though I also offer digital options. Each piece reflects my distinctive art style, combining meticulous line work with creative vision to bring concepts to life.

T-Shirt concept design

I specialize in conceptualizing T-shirt designs, transforming creative ideas into visually striking concepts. My process involves brainstorming, sketching, and refining designs to ensure they effectively capture the intended message and aesthetic.

Charcoal and Graphite Portrait

I create detailed charcoal and graphite portraits, capturing the essence of your loved ones with precision and artistry. Each portrait is crafted using traditional techniques to ensure a timeless and impactful result. These portraits make for unique and meaningful gifts, beautifully preserving cherished moments.

Pin Up art

The term pin-up refers to drawings and paintings, illustrations and photographs of semi-naked women. Using my style and technique, I will be creating a pin up version of your ideas with the model or personality you prefer.

Book And Graphic Novel Materials

I am skilled in creating book and graphic novel materials, with a focus on detailed black-and-white ink illustrations. My expertise lies in producing intricate and expressive artwork that enhances storytelling and visual impact. Each illustration is carefully crafted to bring depth and clarity to both books and graphic novels.

Graphite Illustrations

I create detailed graphite illustrations from client ideas using graphite pencils. After finishing the artwork, I scan it and provide clients with a digital copy.
